Cardlink

A New Way to Network

Bring networking into the digital space with Cardlink.
Exchange information with QR-codes to save interactive digital business cards.
Include your LinkedIn, GitHub, occupation and photograph on an ever-updating networking card.

Developed by Tommy Chang, Alex Blackwell, Katelynn Kuninaka, and Parshan Poubakht.
